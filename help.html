How to use Thirsty?

Thirsty? is an app to quench your thirst! Use Thirsty? to order your drinks online in your nearest coffee shop. Its simple.
First just decide where you want to order your drinks from, Starbucks, Costa Coffee... hmmm... If you're feeling a Starbucks Latte or Machiato, simply click on the Starbucks menu.
You will then see a list of all the possible drinks you can choose from. When you've made your decision, click on the name and you will be taken to the customisation page.
Here you can choose what size of drink you would like, what milk and what syrups you wish to add, don't worry you dont have to add any syrups or milk, its your choice!
Then click Order Now and you will be taken to the Details form.
Here you just fill out your details, so we know its your order, and select the nearest or preferred Starbucks you want to collect your drink at and simple, your order has been made.
